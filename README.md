## 👋🏻 Hi there, I'm Antonio Lupetti 

I am an Italian computer engineer, writer, and independent publisher specializing in mathematics, artificial intelligence, machine learning, and natural language processing.

---

### 🏷️ About Me

Since 2007, I have created and managed several well-established publishing projects. My work has been referenced by international online publications and is characterized by a strong focus on clarity, accuracy, accessibility, and editorial independence.

After an early career at a leading global consulting firm, I went on to hold managerial roles within a major company operating in the aeronautical sector. I was part of the international jury of Awwwards, an organization that recognizes excellence in web design and digital experiences, and I was also included by Wired among the most influential “Under 35” figures on the Italian web.

---

### 💡 Featured Project

In 2023, I founded **[Algebrica](https://algebrica.org)**, a free and open mathematics knowledge base hosted right here on GitHub. 

* **The Vision:** A global reference resource developed independently, without advertising or paywalls, designed for students, educators, and independent learners worldwide.
* **The Tech:** All content is released in Markdown and LaTeX, accompanied by mathematically accurate SVG illustrations designed for educational reuse.
